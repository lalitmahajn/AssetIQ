
# AssetIQ – Pricing Framework (Structure Only)

## Plant Edition
- One‑time license per plant
- Optional annual AMC/support

## Enterprise Edition
- Per‑plant license
- Central HQ fee
- Annual AMC

## Support Tiers
- Standard: business hours
- Premium: extended hours
- Enterprise: 24x7 SLA

## Add‑Ons (Future)
