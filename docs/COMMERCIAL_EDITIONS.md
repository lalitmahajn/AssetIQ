
# AssetIQ – Commercial Editions

## AssetIQ Plant (Single Plant)
**Included**
- Local on‑premise server (1 plant = 1 server)
- Asset registry
- Stop → Ticket → SLA → Report lifecycle
- Email notifications
- Secure audit & append‑only timeline

**Not Included**
- Multi‑plant view
- AI / prediction
- ERP / SAP integration
- Remote control of machines

---

## AssetIQ Enterprise (Multi‑Plant + HQ)
**Included**
- Everything in AssetIQ Plant
- HQ dashboard (read‑only)
- Multi‑plant comparison
- SLA ranking
- Management reports

**Not Included**
- Plant control from HQ
- AI decisions
- Predictive maintenance
